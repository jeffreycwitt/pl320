Common Mistakes

Deduction with Overlapping Quantifiers

Translation With Identity

1. Simple Identity Statements

Superman is Clark Kent

2. Only, The Only, None...Except

Only Joe Biden is President

2. Only, The Only, None...Except

The only person who is president is Joe Biden

2. Only, The Only, None...Except

No one is President except Joe Biden

3. All Except

Everyone except Bob is an Actor

4. Superlative

Bob is the Best Cook

5. Numerical Statement: At most

There at most two people in this class

5. Numerical Statement: At least

There are at least two people in this class

5. Numerical Statement: Exactly

There are exactly two people in this class

6. Definite Description

The Person who stole Christmas was the Grinch
